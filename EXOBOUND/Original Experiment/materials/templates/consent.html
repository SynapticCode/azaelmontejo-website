Consent

Please read this consent agreement carefully before deciding whether to participate in this experiment.

This HIT is part of an MIT scientific research project. Your decision to complete this HIT is voluntary. There is no way for us to identify you. The only information we will have, in addition to your responses, is the time at which you completed the survey. The results of the research may be presented at scientific meetings or published in scientific journals. Clicking on the 'SUBMIT' button on the bottom of this page indicates that you are at least 18 years of age and agree to complete this HIT voluntarily.

Purpose of the research: To examine the way people make judgments and choices.

What you will do in this research: Text and images will be presented on the computer and you will be asked to make choices.

Time required: Participation will take between 10 and 20 minutes to complete.

Risks: The effects of participating should be comparable to those you would experience from viewing a computer monitor for the task duration and using a mouse or keyboard.

Benefits: This study provides no benefits to you individually. The study provides important information about how people make decisions. Others may benefit in the future from the results of this study.

Compensation: You will receive between $1.00 and $6.00 on Amazon Mechanical Turk depending on your choices.

Participation and withdrawal: Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and you may refuse to participate or you may choose to withdraw at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.

How to contact the researcher: If you have questions or concerns about your participation or payment, or want to request a summary of research findings, please contact Rahul Bhui; Email: rbhui@mit.edu; Phone: (617) 253-9250.
